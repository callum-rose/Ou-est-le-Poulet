# Travel challenges — handed out one at a time when the team sets off for the
# next pub, to be done on the walk. Same format as challenges.txt:
#   Title | Description
# Blank lines and lines starting with '#' are ignored. They are handed out in
# order, top to bottom. Organiser: edit these freely.

Street serenade | Sing a chorus of an England football chant loud enough for a stranger to hear on the way. No stopping to do it — keep walking.
Local intel | Ask a passer-by for a pub recommendation before you reach the next stop. Photograph your new friend (with permission).
Bridge toll | Every team member must high-five a different stranger before arriving. Count them out loud.
Statue impression | Find a statue or monument en route and recreate its pose as a team. Photographic proof required.
